What is construction technology?

Construction technology is the use of digital tools — software, mobile apps, cloud platforms, AI and IoT — to plan, monitor and deliver construction projects with greater speed, safety and control. AARAA brings these capabilities together in one platform built for Indian construction and infrastructure firms.
